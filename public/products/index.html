Technology

What is ERP? What is PLM? What is SFA?

ERP

Enterprise resource planning brings together the processes that are essential to running a business. From lines of credit to contract management and accounting. Use Charisma Business Suite to automate your order-to-cash and procure-to-pay operations.

CRM

Customer Relationship Management tracks interactions with customers and business partners. Sales force automation (SFA) and customer support are easy with Charisma Business Suite.

BI

Business intelligence includes the applications used for analyzing information and improving executive decisions. Navigate your way through technology acronyms with our help: choose TotalSoft to get the best in project lifecycle management (PLM), ERP, CRM and BI.
